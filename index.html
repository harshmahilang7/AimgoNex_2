Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Our payment processing is secure and encrypted for your protection.
Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with no penalties.
What happens if I exceed my transaction limit?
Additional transactions are billed at $25 each for Starter and $15 each for Professional plans. Enterprise plans have unlimited transactions.
Do you offer discounts for teams?
Yes, we offer special pricing for teams of 3 or more agents. Contact our sales team for customized team pricing options.
